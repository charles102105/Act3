TECHNOLOGY and EVs

Wireless EV charging

Wireless EV charging, also known as inductive charging, is a cutting-edge technology that allows electric vehicles (EVs) to charge without physical cables. Instead of plugging in, drivers simply park over a charging pad, and the system transfers energy through the air using electromagnetic fields.
Diesel Generator Charging an EV

Diesel generator to charge an EV is a method where electrical power generated from diesel fuel is used to charge the battery of an electric vehicle. While it may sound counterintuitive to use a fossil fuel to power a clean-energy vehicle, it can be useful in specific situations.
Autonomous Charging Robots

A robot charger (or automated EV charging robot) is a smart, robotic system designed to autonomously connect electric vehicles (EVs) to a power source for charging—without human intervention. These systems represent the future of convenience and automation in EV infrastructure charging seamless
